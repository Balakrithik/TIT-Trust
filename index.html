Swarnambikai Sametha Choleeswar Temple (Mettukovil)

The Swarnambikai Sametha Choleeswarar Temple in Oottathur, near Trichy, is a historic Shiva temple dating back to the Chola dynasty. Dedicated to Lord Shiva (Choleeswarar) and Goddess Parvati (Swarnambikai), the temple showcases stunning Dravidian architecture with intricate carvings and a towering vimana. Known for its serene ambiance, it is a spiritual haven for devotees and a testament to Tamil Nadu's rich cultural heritage.
